Long-term Vision

Average student to teacher ratio in the US is around 16:1 with that number significantly higher around the world. By utilizing AIs, teachers will be able to expand their reach without degrading the quality of instruction.

Imagine a hybrid classroom where students and AIs are able to interact with each other as they learn. Through active knowledge construction, the AIs will acquire not only facts but social norms and understanding which will result in their de facto alignment to human values.
